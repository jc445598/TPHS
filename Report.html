Mission

The goal is to propose an efficient, easily accessible and newly upgraded website for Townville Community Music centre to increase the engagement of members in activities e.g. volunteering for administration, organizing and promoting concerts and workshops. It will provide easy access to the users to check the upcoming events and also increase performance opportunities to interested musicians/singers, memberships. One of the goal is also to increase ticket sales, especially to the general public.`
