Mission

The goal is to purpose an efficient, easily accessible and newly upgraded website for Townville Community Music centre to increase the engagement of members in activities e.g. volunteering for administration, organizing and promoting concerts and workshops. It will provide easy access to the users to check the upcoming events, performance opportunities to interested musicians/singers, memberships.

Success Evaluation

The goals are set to be SMART, that is Specific, Measurable, Atainable, Relevant and Time-bound. At the same time certain parameters have been set up to evaluate the success and efficiency of the website. Below are some of the parametrs used for the evaluation.

  • Number of new members associated with the organization in 6 months after the launch of website using Google Analytics.
  • Number of visitors that have visited the website Google Analytics.
  • Number of ne volunteers associated with the organization in 6 months after the launch of website.
  • Number of tickets sold after the launch of website.
  • Feedback from the users.

    • Target Audience

    The target audience will be people with specialized tastes which will include the youth audience and mature audience. The events which include Jazz, Rock and Western music will be dedicated to grabbing the attention of youth audience, whereas as a classical and traditional kind of music events will be conducted to attract the mature audience.
