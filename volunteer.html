Volunteer Singers / Musicians:

The Townsville Concert Band encourages musicians to experience playing in a serious orchestra. Do you play clarinet, flute, oboe or bassoon? French horn? Electric bass? Trombone? Improve your skills in a friendly environment and take part in some major musical events! This is a local community band who meet once a week for rehearsals, with some serious concerts planned throughout the year. The band plays a range of different styles of concert band music, ranging from classical to musical theatre and modern pop. All levels and ages are welcome.
